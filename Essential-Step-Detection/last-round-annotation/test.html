For each (step, goal) pair, you are invited to indicate how essential is the step towards finishing the goal.

  • 2 means that it is very essential. The goal is impossible to be finished without it.
  • 1 means that it is somewhat essential. The step seems to be important, but you are not so sure.
  • 0 means that you do not have a preference.
  • -1 means that it is somewhat not essential. Removing it may have an impact on the overall goal.
  • -2 means that it is not essential at all. Removing it will not influence the overall goal
