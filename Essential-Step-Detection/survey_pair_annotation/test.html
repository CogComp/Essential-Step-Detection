In this task, you are given a process name and a step of completing this process. You are required to determine if a step is essential for completing the process or not. A step is essential if without it, it is impossible to finish the process.

For each provided process that is associated with 5 steps, you need to answer the following three types of questions.

From -2 to 2, you are invited to indicate how essential is each step in your mind.
  • 2 means that it is very essential. The task is impossible to be finished without it.
  • 1 means that it is somewhat essential. The step seems to be important, but you are not so sure.
  • 0 means that you do not have a preference.
  • -1 means that it is somewhat not essential. Removing it may have an impact on the overall process.
  • -2 means that it is not essential at all. Removing it will not influence the overall process

For task "get a Ph.D. degree"

"Defense the dissertation" is an essential step.

For task "get a Ph.D. degree"

"Find an internship" is not an essential step.

On a scale of -2 to 2, how essential is "${step}" towards finishing the goal "${process}"?
